Corpora

What are the texts used to train the model?

The GPT-2 model was trained on 11 different corpora from three main sources.

The Atlantic

Nine of the corpora were web-scraped from The Atlantic and in total amounted to 2014 articles of average length 800 words. The articles were from 2017-2019. The breakdown of the nine corpora is as follows

  • Business: 205 articles and 68 authors
  • Education: 255 articles and 63 authors
  • Entertainment: 234 articles and 68 authors
  • Health: 207 articles and 60 authors
  • Ideas: 252 articles and 123 authors
  • International: 142 articles and 75 authors
  • Politics: 233 articles and 52 authors
  • Science: 257 articles and 59 authors
  • Technology: 229 articles and 57 authors

    • Short Stories

    The Short Story corpus was drawn from all short stories on 100 Great Short Stories, amounting to 160 stories and 52 authors.
