Phantom Pen

Your digital ghostwriter

Phantom Pen is a program that generates original writing using retrained versions of Open AI's Generalized Pre-trained Transformer-2. The program has been re-trained on +2000 essays from The Atlantic, +160 short stories and 20 works of American Literature (see Corpora for a more specific description of the texts used).

There are four choices that enter into the "simple generate" function of the program:

Generator Choices
  • Creativity: The amount of liberty the algorithm will take in generating new text; "1" being the most creative, and "0" leading to repetitive direct copying from corpus.
  • Length: The number of "tokens" the text generator outputs. A proxy for the number of generated words.
  • Class: The corpus (see Corpora) that the text generator will use as a basis for its writing style and content.
  • 117M/345M Model: The chosen model for text generation. The 345M model is more powerful and produces more coherent text, but takes more than twice as long to run.

    Three Functions of the Generator

    Simple Generate


    Generates an article or story of a desired length given an input text and a chosen class.

    ◆ Useful for completing a piece when you begin with a few sentences and a desired class.


    Classify and Generate


    Classifies a given text, states the confidence the classifier has in the classification, and generates an article or story from the corpus associated with the predicted class.

    ◆ Useful for completing a piece when you begin with a few sentences but no chosen class.


    Classify, Extract, and Generate


    Classifies a given text, extracts the key sentences, and uses the key sentences and predicted class to generate an article or story from the corpus associated with the predicted class.

    ◆ Useful for creating an entirely new piece which is topically similar but stylistically different to your original submitted text.
